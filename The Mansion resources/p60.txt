Trixie continues through what seems to be the only other door remaining, but before she enters, she notices a tear in the wall besides the door where a placard might go.

Narrowing her eyes, she manages to see a slight relief of the engraving before, reading ‘Observatory’.

A smaller chamber meets her as she enters, lined with many steps heading upwards in a spiral. She begins to climb them, her previous weariness suddenly alleviating itself with each step of progress.
