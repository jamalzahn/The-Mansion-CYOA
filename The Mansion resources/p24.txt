Trotting ahead, the girls reach the end of the library and face a lone door wedged between two of the tall wooden shelves. As Trixie reaches for the handle, a faint squeal is muffled behind her. Turning, she reels back as a cloud of shadow bursts from the floor, giving her no time to even attempt to save Twilight as she disappears into the floor.

Her ears lowered at the loss of her friend… Trixie heads on alone through the door.

“She’ll come back… she has to”

Trixie whimpers and enters through the door between the wooden shelves.
