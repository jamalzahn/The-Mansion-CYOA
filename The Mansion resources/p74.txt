Trixie canters across the library, looking out through the enormous glass windows between each bookcase, and overlooks the front of the mansion. She notices that is has begun to rain, droplets resting on each of the transparent panels.

At the very end of the room, a large door guarded by two knight statues sits in a thick stone frame. A sense of dread overcomes Trixie as she stares at it, but swallows the feeling and plucks the courage to approach.

The door proves resilient as she tries to open it, but with the aid of her magic, it gives away, and she enters into a large, dark hallway.
